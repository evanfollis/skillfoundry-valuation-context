## Prompt For The Recorder

Capture only what actually happened.

Good evidence notes answer:

- what exact pain did the person describe
- did they distinguish this pain from adjacent categories
- what concrete next-step behavior did they show
- does this support, weaken, or falsify the assumption

## Quality Guidance

- `weak`: polite interest, abstract agreement, no concrete pain or next step
- `moderate`: clear pain recognition or clear distinction from adjacent offers
- `strong`: pain recognition plus concrete movement such as sending materials, asking for terms, or requesting the review

## Support Values

- `supports_assumption`
- `weakens_assumption`
- `falsification_pressure`
