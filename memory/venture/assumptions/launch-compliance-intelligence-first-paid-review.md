**The `falsification_rule` above was never exercised.** The manual-offer probe
made zero external contact, so no builder conversation ever collapsed into
generic copy help — nor did one fail to. The claim is untested through this
channel, not disproven. Anyone reading this later must not treat the park as
evidence against the thesis.

What is actually established: the assumption's `channel_claim` — "the shortest
path to signal is direct outreach" — is the part that failed, and it failed
structurally rather than empirically. Direct outreach requires
principal-initiated contact, which this workspace does not do. A resumed LCI
lane needs a different channel claim, not a different problem claim.
